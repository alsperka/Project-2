who we are

more than clothing

a brand with a message

On Thin Ice is built on the idea that what you wear can say something bigger. Every piece we create is designed to spark conversation about climate change and the urgency of protecting our planet.

why it matters

The effects of climate change are already happening, from rising temperatures to melting ice and disappearing habitats. We use design as a way to bring attention to these issues in everyday life.

what we stand for

Sustainability, awareness, and action. Our products are made with more responsible materials, and a portion of every purchase supports organizations working toward real environmental change.
